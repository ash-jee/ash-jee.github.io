Welcome!

Aishwarya Bhattacharjee

I am an ecologist and conservation biologist, concerned with working at the intersection of science and informed policy for biodiversity conservation, ecosystem services, and effective human-wildlife coexistence. Most recently, I am a PhD candidate at the City University of New York and a National Geographic Explorer, 2019. My doctoral research has focused on vertebrate scavenger communities and the human-scavenger relationship across a vast biome gradient in South Asia.
